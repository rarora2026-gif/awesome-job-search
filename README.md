## Resume & ATS Tools
Tools that score, optimize, or build resumes against Applicant Tracking Systems.
- [Recruit Myself Resume Score](https://recruitmyself.com/resume-score) - Free, instant ATS resume score with actionable fixes. No signup required.
- [Recruit Myself ATS Optimizer](https://recruitmyself.com/ats-optimizer) - Scan a resume against any job description for a keyword-gap analysis.
- [Jobscan](https://www.jobscan.co/) - Resume-to-job-description matching.
- [Teal](https://www.tealhq.com/) - Resume builder plus application tracker.
- [Rezi](https://www.rezi.ai/) - AI resume builder.
- [Resume Worded](https://resumeworded.com/) - Resume and LinkedIn feedback.

## Job Boards & Aggregators
- [Recruit Myself](https://recruitmyself.com/jobs) - AI job board that scores every live role against your CV; hubs for US, UAE, UK, India, Singapore, Canada.
- [LinkedIn Jobs](https://www.linkedin.com/jobs/) - The largest professional network job board.
- [Indeed](https://www.indeed.com/) - Global aggregator.
- [Wellfound](https://wellfound.com/) - Startup jobs (formerly AngelList Talent).
- [Remote OK](https://remoteok.com/) - Remote-only roles.
- [We Work Remotely](https://weworkremotely.com/) - Remote-only roles.

## Interview Preparation
- [Recruit Myself Interview Coach](https://recruitmyself.com/coach) - AI mock interviews and prep.
- [Pramp](https://www.pramp.com/) - Peer mock interviews.
- [LeetCode](https://leetcode.com/) - Technical interview practice.
- [Glassdoor Interviews](https://www.glassdoor.com/) - Company-specific interview questions.

## Salary Research
- [Recruit Myself Salary Guides](https://recruitmyself.com/salary) - Role-level salary data by market.
- [Levels.fyi](https://www.levels.fyi/) - Tech compensation data.
- [Glassdoor](https://www.glassdoor.com/Salaries/) - Crowd-sourced salaries.
- [Payscale](https://www.payscale.com/) - Salary benchmarking.
